GIDEON DRAMA MINISTRY

Join the Drama Ministry

Are you passionate about sharing the Gospel and touching hearts through the power of storytelling? Do you desire to use your creative talents to serve God and inspire others? Then consider joining the drama ministry!

Through drama, you can:

  • Share the message of salvation and hope with a wider audience
  • Bring Bible stories and scriptures to life in an engaging and memorable way
  • Provide a creative outlet for worship and expression
  • Build confidence and teamwork skills
  • Reach people who may not be receptive to traditional preaching methods
  • Create a lasting impact on your community and beyond

In drama ministry, you'll have the opportunity to use your gifts to make a real difference in people's lives. So why not take the leap and join the drama ministry team? Together, let's bring the Gospel to life and inspire others to follow Jesus!
